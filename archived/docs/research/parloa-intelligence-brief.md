## Executive Summary

Parloa is a Berlin-founded enterprise voice AI and conversational AI platform that has rapidly scaled to a $3 billion valuation with $562 million in total funding. Founded in 2018 by Malte Kosub (CEO) and Stefan Ostwald (CTO/CPO), the company builds an AI Agent Management Platform (AMP) for contact centres, specialising in voice-first AI agents that automate phone, chat, and messaging interactions for Global 2000 enterprises. Their sweet spot is large B2C companies in insurance, retail, travel, and telecom with high call volumes and legacy IVR pain. The company surpassed $50M ARR in 2025, serves 150+ enterprise customers across 1,000+ brand deployments, and is aggressively expanding from its European base into North America.

## 2. Product & Capabilities

### Platform: AI Agent Management Platform (AMP)

Parloa's core product is a lifecycle management platform for AI agents deployed in contact centres. The platform covers four stages:

1. **Design** -- Parloa Studio (low-code visual builder), pre-built Skills library, custom Skills development, enterprise integration connectors
2. **Test** -- Simulation tools, evaluation frameworks, version control for conversation flows
3. **Deploy/Scale** -- Multi-channel deployment (voice, chat, WhatsApp, Apple Messages, Facebook, click-to-call, multimodal), model orchestration, agent composition
4. **Monitor/Optimize** -- Insights dashboards, Data Hub, Conversation Store for historical analysis

### Key Capabilities

| Capability | Detail |
|-----------|--------|
| **Voice AI** | Voice-first architecture; cloud telephony integrations; natural-sounding agents |
| **Multilingual** | Real-time translation in 35+ languages; native language models (not just translation layers) |
| **Omnichannel** | Phone, chat, WhatsApp, Apple Messages, Facebook Messenger, web, mobile apps |
| **LLM Integration** | Azure OpenAI, OpenAI, Google Cloud, BYO STT/TTS/LLM |
| **Agent Assist** | Real-time support for human agents during live calls |
| **Multimodal** | Blends voice, video, chat, images, interactive widgets |
| **Conversational Memory** | Multi-turn reasoning with context retention |
| **Auto-Failover** | Built-in redundancy between Azure and OpenAI; auto-reroutes on performance dips |

### Enterprise Integrations

- **CCaaS:** Genesys, Twilio, AWS Connect, Cisco

### Known Limitations

- **Deployment time:** 1-3 months typical (vs. some competitors claiming 3 weeks)
- **Technical complexity:** Requires developer involvement, scripting logic, CRM-specific data models
- **No voice cloning:** Limited voice customisation (tone/speed only)
- **No self-serve:** No free trial, no startup tier, no public pricing
- **Support model:** Assigned CSM only; no live chat or public ticketing system
