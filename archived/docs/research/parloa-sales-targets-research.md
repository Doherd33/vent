## Executive Summary

Parloa is positioned at the intersection of two massive trends: the death of legacy IVR and the rise of agentic AI in contact centres. The European CCaaS market is growing at 19.7% CAGR to $7.53B by 2032, and 9 out of 10 global contact centres plan to increase self-service investment. The targets below are selected for high inbound call volume, known customer service pain points, active digital transformation programmes, and alignment with Parloa's DACH/European home market advantage.

## TELECOMS (Highest pain, highest volume)

### 1. Vodafone Group
- **Website:** vodafone.com
- **What they do:** One of the world's largest telecoms, operating in 21 countries with 300M+ mobile customers and 28M+ fixed broadband customers across Europe and Africa.
- **Why Parloa fits:** Vodafone consistently tops UK complaint tables (Ofcom Q2 and Q3 2025 data). Their existing AI assistant "TOBi" handles basic queries but is widely criticised for inability to resolve complex issues. The recent Vodafone-Three UK merger (VodafoneThree) creates massive integration complexity and increased call volumes. They announced bringing 400+ contact jobs back to the UK in Dec 2025 -- a sign they are rethinking their CX model.
- **Sales entry point:** Group CTO / VP Digital Customer Experience. Angle: "Replace TOBi's limitations with Parloa's agentic AI that actually resolves issues end-to-end, reducing the 400 new hires needed."
- **Urgency signal:** VodafoneThree merger integration underway; Ofcom complaint leadership in Q3 2025; new Google Cloud agentic architecture partnership announced but not yet deployed to customer service.

### 2. Deutsche Telekom
- **Website:** telekom.com

Their consumer strategy explicitly relies on AI in customer service. However, they are building bespoke solutions rather than using a purpose-built platform -- creating technical debt and slow time-to-value. DACH home market means Parloa has a geographic and regulatory advantage.
- **Sales entry point:** SVP Customer Experience / Head of AI & Automation, T-Systems. Angle: "Purpose-built AI agent platform vs. DIY -- faster deployment, enterprise-grade reliability, the 'Parloa Promise' SLA."
- **Urgency signal:** MWC 2026 AI strategy presentation; OpenAI partnership for co-creating AI products; actively building but not yet deployed conversational AI at scale.

- **Sales entry point:** COO / Head of Customer Service. Angle: "Handle the freenet migration support surge and new mobile customer onboarding without proportionally scaling headcount."
