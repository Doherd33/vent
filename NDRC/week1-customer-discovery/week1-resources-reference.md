## Frameworks & Guides

### Toolbox Personas
Template for building detailed customer personas — demographics, goals, frustrations, archetypes. We've built our own Vent-specific versions in `NDRC/personas/`.

### Empathy Map
A visual framework for understanding customers across six dimensions: Think, Feel, See, Hear, Say, Do. Goes deeper than personas into the emotional experience. We've built empathy maps for Ciaran (upstream) and Aoife (downstream) in `NDRC/personas/`.

### The Mom Test
A guide for conducting customer interviews that uncover genuine insights without bias or wishful thinking. The core rule: never ask "would you use this?" Instead, ask about their actual behaviour, problems, and workarounds. Essential reading before any customer conversation.

### The Opportunity Signal: How to Pick the Right Customer Segment
By Daniel Kyne. A framework for narrowing down which customer segment to target first. For Vent: should we start with CDMOs, mid-size biopharma, large multinationals, or single-product sites? This helps answer that.

### How to Validate New Product Ideas (The Data-Driven, No-BS Way)
By Daniel Kyne. Goes deep on the "customer discovery sandwich" — a structured approach to validating that what you're building is something people will pay for. Combines qualitative interviews with quantitative signals.

### 26 Resources to Help You Master Customer Development Interviews
By Neil Patel. A compilation of interview techniques, question frameworks, and best practices for getting real signal from customer conversations.

## Lead Generation & Outreach Tools

### Dux Soup
Automated LinkedIn lead generation tool. Visits profiles, sends connection requests, and manages outreach sequences. Useful when we're ready to find and reach pharma quality managers, ops directors, and engineering leads on LinkedIn.

### ZoomInfo
Lead generation and prospecting database with deep contact information — emails, phone numbers, org charts. Useful for identifying decision-makers at target pharma companies (heads of manufacturing, VP operations, quality directors).

### Tally Forms
Free online form builder (like Typeform or Jotform). Useful for building quick customer discovery surveys — e.g., "How long does it take you to find an SOP on the floor?" Send to pharma contacts to gather quantitative data alongside interviews.

## Sales & Growth

### The Cold Email Manifesto
A system for predictable lead generation through cold email. Covers finding leads, writing effective emails, and building a repeatable outreach process. For later — when we're ready to do targeted outreach to pharma manufacturing sites.
