The Problem
The floor knows.
The facility doesn't.

In regulated biologics manufacturing, the operators closest to the process are the first to spot deviations, inefficiencies, and procedure gaps. Almost none of it gets captured in time to be useful.

🚨
Reporting is feared

Deviation systems are traceable. Operators know that raising an issue means their name is attached. So observations go unreported, and the facility operates blind.

📋
Documentation consumes the day

30–40% of facility man-hours go to documentation. SOPs live in PDFs nobody reads proactively. Deviations are filed weeks after they happen. The signal is always delayed.

📉
Signal degrades at every handoff

By the time an observation reaches QA or management, it has been filtered, delayed, and summarised. The original insight , the one that could prevent the next deviation , is gone.

"The same deviation happens three times across three shifts before anyone connects the dots , because the system isn't designed to connect them."

, A reality observed daily on biologics manufacturing floors

Compliance Architecture
Regulation-ready by design.

Most QMS tools bolt compliance on as an afterthought. Vent is built for regulated environments from the first line of code , because it was designed by someone who lives those requirements.

21 CFR PART 11 / EU ANNEX 11

Electronic signatures with user ID and justification required on all approval transitions , QA sign-off, director approval, closure, rejection.

IMMUTABLE AUDIT TRAIL

Every action , submission, status change, SOP modification, CAPA update, login , logged with SHA-256 checksums, timestamps, IP addresses, and user agents.

ALCOA+ DATA INTEGRITY

Data is Attributable, Legible, Contemporaneous, Original, and Accurate. Anonymity for operators does not compromise traceability for the system , these are separated by design.

ROLE-BASED ACCESS CONTROL

Six distinct roles (Operator, QA, Director, MSAT, Engineering, Admin) with fine-grained route-level enforcement. Sensitive transitions locked by role.

DATABASE-LEVEL SECURITY

Supabase Row Level Security policies. No direct table access without authentication. All queries proxy through the authenticated Express server.

Why this matters for customers

FDA and HPRA inspectors audit electronic records systems. A platform without a compliant audit architecture cannot enter a GMP facility , full stop. Vent is inspection-ready from day one, which removes the single biggest procurement blocker.
